Principles

How the Labs differs from the main site

Fast to publish

No build complexity in the Labs surface itself. Just static files and ideas that can move quickly.

Allowed to be unfinished

Not everything here needs a product-shaped ending. Exploration is a valid output.

Meant to cross-pollinate

Good experiments can become posts, product concepts, project pages, or better questions.
